Local Handyman Questions

Oakland County Handyman FAQ

Quick answers for homeowners comparing local repair, maintenance, and small project help.

What handyman services do you offer?

AAA Handyman Services handles carpentry and trim, doors, drywall, painting, flooring, deck and fence repair, maintenance, cleaning, and minor plumbing or electrical repairs.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Waterford, Michigan and serve Oakland County communities including Pontiac, West Bloomfield, Bloomfield Hills, White Lake, Commerce Township, Clarkston, Lake Orion, Rochester Hills, Troy, Royal Oak, Birmingham, Southfield, Farmington Hills, Milford, Highland, and nearby areas.

Do you offer emergency handyman service?

Yes. After-hours emergency services are available 7 days a week for urgent repairs such as leaks or door and window problems that affect home safety or security. Senior services are also available, including practical home modifications and safety upgrades that help you or your loved ones live independently and confidently.

How much does handyman service cost?

We use a Fair Upfront Pricing Model starting with a Minimum Service Call of $100 for Zone A (within 20 miles) or $145 for Zone B (outside 20 miles), which covers travel, diagnostics, and includes up to the first hour of labor. Continuous labor after the first hour is billed in transparent half-hour increments at a flat rate of $65 per hour. Materials are not included in the hourly rate — supply them yourself at no markup, or have us source them with a standard 10–15% supply markup.
