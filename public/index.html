Carpentry & Trim

Revive your home’s character. From baseboards and crown molding to custom exterior woodwork, I provide precision craftsmanship that restores the aesthetic and structural integrity of your living space.

Doors

Secure, smooth, and seamless. Whether it’s an elegant new entry door for curb appeal or an interior door that sticks, I handle expert installation and alignment to ensure your doors function perfectly every time.

Minor Plumbing Repairs

Stop the drip, protect your home. I provide fast, clean solutions for leaky faucets, showerheads, and stubborn drains, ensuring your home’s flow is restored efficiently.

Drywall

Seamless surfaces, flawless finishes. Don’t let cracks or holes distract from your home’s interior. I deliver craftsman-grade patching and smoothing, leaving your walls paint-ready and pristine.

Minor Electrical Services

Safe, reliable updates. Keep your home powered and bright. I specialize in replacing outdated switches, outlets, and fixtures or installing new ceiling fans with safety and code-conscious precision. Flooring Solutions

Durable beauty underfoot. From tile and hardwood to laminate and carpet, I provide expert maintenance and repair services, ensuring your floors are level, secure, and built to last.

Painting

A fresh look, perfectly executed. Painting is 90% preparation. I focus on meticulous surface prep, crisp edging, and smooth, craftsman finishes to revitalize your home's appearance inside and out.

Decks & Fences

Restore your outdoor sanctuary. Extend the life of your wood structures with craftsman resurfacing, structural repairs, and expert staining to protect your investment against the elements.

Maintenance & Cleaning

Local Handyman Questions

Oakland County Handyman FAQ

Quick answers for homeowners comparing local repair, maintenance, and small project help.

What handyman services do you offer?

AAA Handyman Services handles carpentry and trim, doors, drywall, painting, flooring, deck and fence repair, maintenance, cleaning, and minor plumbing or electrical repairs.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Waterford, Michigan and serve Oakland County communities including Pontiac, West Bloomfield, Bloomfield Hills, White Lake, Commerce Township, Clarkston, Lake Orion, Rochester Hills, Troy, Royal Oak, Birmingham, Southfield, Farmington Hills, Milford, Highland, and nearby areas.

Do you offer emergency handyman service?

After-hours emergency services are available 7 days a week for urgent repairs such as leaks or door and window problems that affect home safety or security.

How much does handyman service cost?

We use a Fair Upfront Pricing Model starting with a Minimum Service Call of $120 for Zone A (within 20 miles) or $165 for Zone B (outside 20 miles), which covers travel, diagnostics, and includes up to the first hour of labor. Consecutive labor after the first hour is billed in transparent half-hour increments at a flat rate of $65 per hour. Materials are not included in the hourly rate.
