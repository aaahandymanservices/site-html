Local Handyman Questions

AAA Handyman Services FAQ

Got questions about our services, flat rates, audits, menus, packages, or coverage? Find clear, direct answers below.

What is AAA Handyman Services?

AAA Handyman Services is a professional local handyman company specializing in reliable minor home repairs, maintenance, and small-scale projects. Under Victor Gregg Hale's ownership, we serve homeowners across Oakland County, Michigan. We guarantee high-quality and transparently priced craftsmanship.

How do your standard hourly rates work?

We use a Fair Upfront Pricing Model with zero hidden fees. Your visit begins with a Minimum Service Call of $100 for Zone A (within 20 miles) or $145 for Zone B (20+ miles), covering travel, diagnosis, and the first hour of labor. Any continuous labor after the first hour is billed in transparent 15-minute increments at a flat rate of $70 per hour. Materials are billed separately at a 10–15% standard markup, or you can supply them yourself at zero markup!

Do you offer flat-rate pricing menus?

Yes! For common, straightforward repairs, we provide flat-rate menu items. This gives you absolute certainty over costs before we even begin. Flat-rate items include straightforward installations (like deadbolts, faucets, ceiling fans, or smart doorbells), drywall patches, TV mounting, and gutter cleanings. Check our full Rates page for a detailed pricing menu.

What are your specialized audits?

We offer three high-value specialized audits designed to inspect, diagnose, and secure your home:
1. Home Safety Audit ($99): Fall prevention, detector checks, trip hazards, and entry security.
2. Energy Tune-Up Audit ($99): Detect and seal minor on-the-spot drafts or air leaks.
3. Storm-Prep Check ($99): Exterior caulk seals, drainage, and gutter inspection.
Note: Audit fees are credited toward same-day repairs over $250.

Do you offer service packages and add-ons?

We do! Our 4-hour, 6-hour, and 8-hour Handyman Time Packages help homeowners knock out a punch list in one scheduled visit. We also offer service add-ons such as dryer vent cleaning, smart lock setup, and smoke detector battery replacement that can be added to a standard service call.

What are your service areas?

We are based in Waterford, Michigan, and primarily serve Oakland County.
Zone A (Standard - Within 20 miles): Waterford, Clarkston, West Bloomfield, Troy, Rochester Hills, Pontiac, Orchard Lake, Farmington Hills, Franklin, Beverly Hills, Birmingham, Novi, Lake Orion, and Oxford.
Zone B (Extended - Outside 20 miles): Royal Oak, Huntington Woods, Southfield, and surrounding communities.
