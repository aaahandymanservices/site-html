Professional Services

No job is to small, we've got you covered with competitive rates.

Carpentry & Trim

Revive your home’s character. From baseboards and crown molding to custom exterior woodwork, I provide precision craftsmanship that restores the aesthetic and structural integrity of your living space.

Doors

Secure, smooth, and seamless. Whether it’s an elegant new entry door for curb appeal or an interior door that sticks, I handle expert installation and alignment to ensure your doors function perfectly every time.

Minor Plumbing Repairs

Stop the drip, protect your home. I provide fast, clean solutions for leaky faucets, showerheads, and stubborn drains, ensuring your home’s flow is restored efficiently.

Drywall

Seamless surfaces, flawless finishes. Don’t let cracks or holes distract from your home’s interior. I deliver professional-grade patching and smoothing, leaving your walls paint-ready and pristine.

Minor Electrical Services

Safe, reliable updates. Keep your home powered and bright. I specialize in replacing outdated switches, outlets, and fixtures or installing new ceiling fans with safety and code-conscious precision. (Note: I focus on fixtures and safety updates; new wiring or breaker box installations are not included.)

Flooring Solutions

Durable beauty underfoot. From tile and hardwood to laminate and carpet, I provide expert maintenance and repair services, ensuring your floors are level, secure, and built to last.

Painting

A fresh look, perfectly executed. Painting is 90% preparation. I focus on meticulous surface prep, crisp edging, and smooth, professional finishes to revitalize your home's appearance inside and out.

Decks & Fences

Restore your outdoor sanctuary. Extend the life of your wood structures with professional resurfacing, structural repairs, and expert staining to protect your investment against the elements.

Maintenance & Cleaning

Seasonal care, year-round peace of mind. Cross off your to-do list with comprehensive gutter cleaning, power washing, and general home maintenance designed to keep your property in peak condition.
