Energy-Efficiency Tune-Up in Waterford, Troy, West Bloomfield & Oakland County, MI

Comfort up, energy bills down. Drafts around doors and windows quietly drive up your heating and cooling costs all year. We replace worn weatherstripping and door sweeps, seal hidden air leaks, and close the small gaps that waste conditioned air, so your home stays comfortable and efficient.

A focused tune-up is one of the highest-return improvements you can make, paying for itself in lower utility bills and a more comfortable home.

Energy-Efficiency Tune-Up FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer Energy-Efficiency Tune-Up in Oakland County, MI?

Yes. AAA Handyman Services is based in Waterford and provides energy-efficiency tune-up services for homeowners throughout Oakland County, Michigan, from Waterford and Clarkston to Troy, Royal Oak, Novi, and the surrounding communities.

How much does energy-efficiency tune-up cost?

Pricing starts with a minimum service call: $100 in Zone A (within about 20 miles of our Waterford base) or $145 in Zone B (extended county, 20+ miles). That covers travel, diagnostics, and up to the first hour of labor. Continuous labor after the first hour is billed at a flat $70 per hour in quarter-hour increments, and materials are billed separately.

How do I know if my home needs an energy tune-up?

Cold drafts near doors and windows, rooms that never feel the right temperature, and rising utility bills are all signs of air leaks. We can walk your home, find the leaks, and seal the ones that matter most in a single visit.

Do you offer emergency service for urgent issues?

Yes. After-hours emergency service is available 7 days a week for urgent repairs that affect home safety or security, such as leaks or door and window problems. Call (248) 385-3432 for priority response.
