2 Licensing & Scope of Work

AAA Handyman Services specializes in minor home repairs, maintenance, and small-scale improvement projects. We are not a licensed residential builder or general contractor.

Michigan Minor Project Exemption

We operate under the exemption in Michigan's Occupational Code, MCL 339.2403, which allows unlicensed home repair and improvement work for minor projects where the total contract value (including labor, materials, and all fees) remains strictly under $600. We accept only projects that fall within the legal parameters of this exemption. Larger jobs, and any work that would require a residential builder or general contractor license, are referred to an appropriately licensed professional.
  • We do not divide larger projects into multiple smaller contracts in order to circumvent licensing requirements. Each project we accept is a genuinely separate undertaking matching our scope of minor repairs.
  • Plumbing, electrical, and mechanical work require the appropriate skilled-trade license under Michigan law regardless of price. Our services in these areas are limited to minor tasks that an unlicensed person may lawfully perform; any work requiring a licensed trade is performed or subcontracted by an appropriately licensed contractor.
  • Where a project requires a permit or licensed trade, we will let you know before work begins so it can be arranged properly.

3 Rates & Pricing

  • We offer honest, upfront, transparent pricing. Jobs are priced either on an hourly basis with a flat Minimum Service Call, as a prepaid time package, as a flat-fee audit package, or by the bid (a fixed price for an agreed scope).
  • Standard service calls include travel, diagnostics, and up to the first hour of labor: $100 Minimum Service Call in Zone A (within ~20 miles of our Waterford base) and $145 Minimum Service Call in Zone B (extended Oakland County area). Continuous labor after the first hour is billed at a flat $70/hour in quarter-hour increments.
  • We offer half-day and full-day prepaid time packages: 4-Hour Package ($310), 6-Hour Package ($450), and 8-Hour Package ($580) for tackling multi-item punch lists in a single visit.
  • Flat-fee inspection packages—including our Home Safety Audit ($99), Energy Tune-Up Audit ($99), Storm-Prep Check ($99), or Whole-Home Assessment 3-Audit Bundle ($249)—include room-by-room assessments and a same-day line-item punch list. The audit fee is credited directly toward same-day repairs totaling over $250.
  • We provide free quotes online or by phone. To receive a fast and accurate estimate, we encourage you to contact us with your project details and, if possible, photos of the work area.
  • Materials are not included in the standard labor rate. You may supply materials yourself at no markup, or we can source them on your behalf, in which case a standard 10–15% supply markup applies to cover sourcing time, transport, and parts liability.
  • Priority after-hours and emergency service is available 7 days a week (Zone A urgent rate: $150 minimum service call / $95/hr after; Zone B urgent rate: $195 minimum / $95/hr after). Current figures are always listed on our Rates page.
  • Payment is due upon completion of the day's work unless other arrangements have been agreed upon in writing beforehand. We accept Cash, Check, Zelle®, and Venmo®.

4 Scheduling, Online Booking & Cancellations

  • We strive to accommodate your preferred schedule and complete work efficiently.
  • You can submit a service request through our online booking portal. Submitting a request is an application for an appointment, not a confirmed booking, until we review details and contact you to confirm the date, time, and scope.
  • Homeowners can voluntarily subscribe to our free seasonal maintenance reminder service on our booking or contact forms to receive periodic maintenance check reminders (e.g., spring gutter checks, fall weatherproofing). You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • 24-hour notice is appreciated for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a trip charge equal to the applicable Minimum Service Call.
  • Prepaid time packages follow the same notice policy; a missed visit rescheduled with less than 24-hour notice may forfeit the reserved time.
  • After-hours and emergency requests are accommodated 7 days a week when a craftsman is available and are billed at the urgent rates described in Section 3.
