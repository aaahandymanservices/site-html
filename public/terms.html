2 Licensing & Scope of Work

AAA Handyman Services specializes in minor home repairs, maintenance, and small-scale improvement projects. We are not a licensed residential builder or general contractor.

Michigan Minor Project Exemption

We operate under the exemption in Michigan's Occupational Code, MCL 339.2403, which allows unlicensed home repair and improvement work for minor projects. We accept only projects that fall within the legal parameters of this exemption. Larger jobs, and any work that would require a residential builder or general contractor license, are referred to an appropriately licensed professional.
  • We do not divide larger projects into multiple smaller contracts in order to circumvent licensing requirements. Each project we accept is a genuinely separate undertaking matching our scope of minor repairs.
  • Plumbing, electrical, and mechanical work require the appropriate skilled-trade license under Michigan law regardless of price. Our services in these areas are limited to minor tasks that an unlicensed person may lawfully perform; any work requiring a licensed trade is performed or subcontracted by an appropriately licensed contractor.
  • Where a project requires a permit or licensed trade, we will let you know before work begins so it can be arranged properly.

  • We offer highly competitive rates tailored to your specific project needs. Jobs are priced either by the bid (a fixed price for the entire project) or on an hourly basis, ensuring honest, upfront, and transparent pricing with no surprises.
