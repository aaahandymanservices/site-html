Flat-Fee Home Audits

Not sure where to start? Book a flat-fee audit and put a trained craftsman's eyes on your home. Each audit is a focused, hands-on inspection that ends with a same-day punch-list — every recommended fix written down with its own line-item price, so you can approve exactly what you want on the spot. The audit fee is credited back toward any same-day repairs over $250.

To ensure compliance with Michigan's minor project exemption (MCL 339.2403), same-day repair contracts are limited to a maximum of $600, including labor and materials. Audit credits are applied directly to your invoice on eligible same-day projects between $250 and $600.

How It Works

  1. 1Book the flat-fee audit. Pick the audit that fits — or bundle all three — and reserve a time that works for you.
  2. 2Walk the home together. We inspect hands-on and note every issue worth fixing, big or small.
  3. 3Get your priced punch-list. Each fix is written down with a clear line-item price — no vague ranges, no pressure.
  4. 4Approve and we start. Green-light any items and we knock them out the same day. Your audit fee credits toward same-day work over $250.

Audit fees shown are Zone A (within 20 miles); Zone B adds our standard $45 extended-travel differential. The audit fee is credited toward same-day repairs totaling $250 or more.
