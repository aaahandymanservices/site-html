Parts & Materials

Materials & Supply Runs

Materials are never bundled into your Service Call or hourly rate. You always have two transparent options — supply them yourself, or let us handle the sourcing for you.

You Supply the Materials

Have the parts ready on-site and there is no markup at all. We simply install what you provide. Please confirm the exact make, model, and specs before your appointment so nothing stalls the job.

$0 materials markup

We Supply the Materials

Skip the store run. With prior agreement we shop, select, and warranty the correct parts. A standard 10–15% supply markup covers our sourcing time, vehicle use, and the liability of standing behind every part we bring.

10–15% supply markup

Prefer to pay for time instead of a percentage? Dedicated supply runs can be billed under our $65/hr Continuous Labor rate in half-hour increments — just note that the hourly option covers our time on the run only. Unlike the percentage markup, it does not include the parts warranty, travel, or handling of returns and exchanges; those are bundled into the 10–15% markup. Materials we purchase under that markup carry our workmanship warranty; parts you supply are never covered.
