Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

At AAA Handyman Services, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website aaahandyman.services and use our handyman and home improvement services in Oakland County, Michigan.

We are a locally owned and operated business dedicated to reliable, honest, and quality service. Your privacy is important to us. Please read this policy carefully.

1 Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact details (name, phone number, email, address)
  • Service request details and project descriptions
  • Payment details processed securely
  • Communication records and project photos

Automatically Collected Information

  • Website usage details (IP address, pages visited, browser type)
  • Device specifications and operating system information

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, schedule, and complete requested handyman services.
  • To communicate about appointments, estimates, and follow-ups.
  • To process payments and send receipts.
  • To improve our services and overall website experience.
  • To send occasional updates about promotions or seasonal maintenance tips.

Text Messaging

If you provide your mobile number, we may contact you by call or text about appointments, estimates, and service follow-ups. We send promotional text messages only with your express consent. Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies. You can opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP, or opt out of promotional messages by contacting us using the details below. Opting out of promotional messages does not affect service-related messages about a job you have booked.

3 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited cases:

  • With trusted service partners or subcontractors needed to complete your project.
  • With secure payment processors and essential web analytics providers.
  • When required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our customers and team.

4 Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand visitor behavior and improve our site. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5 Data Security

Security Measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6 Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to request access to or correction of your personal information, ask us to delete your data (subject to legal requirements), and opt out of promotional communications. Contact us using the information below to exercise these rights.

7 Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from anyone under 13. If we discover such information, we will delete it promptly.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
